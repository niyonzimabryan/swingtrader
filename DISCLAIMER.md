# Financial Disclaimer

Swing Trader is research and paper-trading software. It is not financial, investment, tax, or legal advice.

## Paper trading first

This project is built for paper trading and research workflows. Do not connect it to live brokerage credentials unless you understand the code, the strategy, the broker integration, and the risk controls.

## No unsupervised live trading

Swing Trader is not intended for unattended live trading. Model outputs can be wrong, stale, incomplete, or misread by downstream code. Market conditions can change faster than the system reacts.

## Human review required

An operator must review every trade before approval. Treat every memo as a research aid, not as an instruction to buy, sell, short, hold, or size a position.
